LOST FOREST

Lost Forest is a lush, tropical area within the zoo that simulates a rainforest environment. It features dense vegetation, exotic plants, and a variety of animal species that thrive in such habitats. Visitors can explore winding paths, cross wooden bridges, and observe animals like monkeys, birds, and reptiles in their naturalistic settings.

The Lost Forest is designed to educate visitors about the importance of rainforest ecosystems and the conservation efforts needed to protect these vital habitats. It provides an immersive experience, allowing guests to feel as if they have stepped into a real rainforest, complete with the sounds of nature and the sights of vibrant flora and fauna.
