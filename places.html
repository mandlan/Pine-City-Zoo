Animals Map Places

INSECT HOUSE

Insect House is a fascinating exhibit showcasing a variety of insects from around the world, including butterflies, beetles, and ants.

MONKEY TRAIL

Monkey Trail is a winding path through the zoo that allows visitors to observe various species of monkeys in their naturalistic habitats.

AMPTHEATHER

The Amphitheater is a large outdoor venue within the zoo where educational shows and presentations about animals are held regularly.

WILD THINGS COFFE SHOP

Wild Things Coffee Shop is a cozy spot within the zoo where visitors can enjoy refreshments and snacks while taking a break from exploring.

LOST FOREST

Lost Forest is a lush, immersive area of the zoo designed to mimic a tropical rainforest, featuring exotic plants and animals.

Mo'S PIZZA

Mo's Pizza is a popular eatery within the zoo, offering a variety of delicious pizzas and other Italian dishes for visitors to enjoy.
