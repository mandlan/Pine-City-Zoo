AMPTHEATHER

The Ampitheater is a large outdoor venue within the Pine City Zoo, designed to host various events and performances. It provides a scenic backdrop for concerts, educational talks, and animal shows, allowing visitors to enjoy entertainment in a natural setting.

With its spacious seating and open-air design, the Ampitheater is a popular spot for families and friends to gather and experience live performances while surrounded by the beauty of the zoo's environment.
