WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Wild Things Coffee Shop is a cozy spot within the Pine City Zoo, offering a variety of coffee beverages, teas, and light snacks for visitors to enjoy. It's the perfect place to take a break and recharge while exploring the zoo.

Located near the main entrance, the coffee shop provides a welcoming atmosphere with comfortable seating and a view of the zoo's lush surroundings. Whether you're looking for a quick caffeine fix or a place to relax, Wild Things Coffee Shop has you covered.
