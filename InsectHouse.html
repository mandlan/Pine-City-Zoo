INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is a fascinating exhibit at the Pine City Zoo, showcasing a diverse range of insects from around the world. Visitors can explore various habitats and learn about the unique adaptations and behaviors of these small but essential creatures.

From colorful butterflies to industrious ants, the Insect House provides an educational and engaging experience for all ages, highlighting the importance of insects in our ecosystem.
